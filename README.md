# KWC---Knightfall-Wanderers-Chronicles
Everything about my minecraft modpack focused around PvP, PvE and adventure in a medieval fantastic world; Knightfall!

# Welcome to the Knightfall Wanderers Chronicles!

## What is it?
In this modpack, you will be set in a medieval and fantastic world filled with equipment, structures, quests and enemies to fight. It features great landscapes as well as a very immersive atmosphere.
![Beautifull landscape offered by 'Lithosphere' and 'Still Life'](https://imgur.com/BBpfbmz)

## In which ways can I play it?
In solo or with friends (if you use the Essential mod or host you own server). I still recommend multiplayer as it allows for longer playtime, more dynamic during fights and most importantly: more **fun**!

![Mountains](https://imgur.com/4GO1077)


_If you do decide to host your own server, I recommend pregenerating the landscape and limiting the world to a max of 10 000 x 10 000._
